Security

Having a strong password is vital to keeping your information private. The National Institute of Standards and Technology suggests having longer passwords, with a mix of upper case, lower case, special characters, and numbers. For example, "password" can be improved using these listed password security measures to be "!MyPassworD$". These slight changes in your base password could greatly reduce the chances of having your account hacked.
